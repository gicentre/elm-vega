Used for creating multiple views in a single specification. Faceting is the process of extracting different parts of a dataset ('facets') and applying the same or similar visualization specification to each.

Anscombe's Quartet

The Trellis display by Becker et al. helped establish small multiples as a "powerful mechanism for understanding interactions in studies of how a response depends on explanatory variables". Here reproduced as a trellis of Barley yields from the 1930s, complete with main-effects ordering to facilitate comparison.

Stock prices of four large companies as a small multiples of area charts.
